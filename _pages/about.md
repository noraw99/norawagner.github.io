My research focuses on clustering algorithms, Galactic dynamics and young stellar populations. 

## Master’s Thesis on nearby Clustering
For my Master's thesis in Computational Science, I developed a (deep) clustering algorithm aimed at identifying nearby co-moving groups of stars that are hard to find due to projection effects. 

## Master’s Thesis on Carina
My Master's thesis in Astronomy concentrated on the Carina association, utilizing Gaia DR3 data to analyze the age and the dynamics of this young star cluster.
